Applicant Information
Membership Level

NP Mebership (Non-Profit)

Benefits:

  • Access to networking events and workshops.
  • Listing in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Promotion of events and news through the Chamber's social media channels.
  • Opportunity to collaborate with other non-profit organizations.

Requirements:

  • Must be a registered non-profit organization.
  • Provide proof of non-profit status.
  • Commitment to the Chamber's mission and values.

Bronze Membership

Benefits:

  • All benefits of NP Membership, plus:
  • Increased exposure through featured listings on the Chamber's website.
  • Discounts on advertising and sponsorship opportunities.
  • Access to exclusive industry reports and market insights.

Requirements:

  • Pay the designated membership fee.
  • Provide a brief description of the business or organization.
  • Agree to abide by the Chamber's code of conduct.

Silver Membership

Benefits:

  • All benefits of Bronze Membership, plus:
  • Priority access to Chamber events and programs.
  • Enhanced visibility through prominent logo placement on Chamber materials.
  • Exclusive invitations to industry-specific workshops and seminars.
  • Access to member-only resources and business support services.

Requirements:

  • Pay the designated membership fee, higher than the Bronze level.
  • Demonstrate a commitment to business growth and community engagement.
  • Provide testimonials or references from other Chamber members.

Gold Membership

Benefits:

  • All benefits of Silver Membership, plus:
  • Premium listing in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities at Chamber events.
  • Personalized business advisory services from Chamber experts.
  • Invitations to high-level networking events and executive roundtables.

Requirements:

  • Pay the designated membership fee, higher than the Silver level.
  • Show a strong track record of business success and community involvement.
  • Participate in Chamber committees or leadership roles.
