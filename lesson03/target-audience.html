Target Audience

The target group of the Lo Campino Chamber of Commerce website are local companies, entrepreneurs, professionals and young professionals, and everyone interested in personal and Lo Campino economic development and growth. The target audience is people who are looking for information, resources and networking opportunities in order to improve their businesses or explore investment and educational opportunities within the city.

Person 1: Businessman - Roberto Sanchez

  • Age: 40
  • Occupation: Owner of a small transport company in Lo Campino
  • Married and father of 4 children.
  • Engineer in business administration.
  • Goals: Increase the visibility of your business, explore marketing strategies, access business support programs, and meet and connect with other local business owners to collaborate and seek opportunities to promote your business.

Person 2: Marketing Consultant - Lucas Tapia

  • Age: 28
  • Occupation: Recently graduated independent Marketing Consultant.
  • Single.
  • He lives alone in an apartment.
  • Objectives: Establish contacts with local companies in search of marketing consulting services. Get additional marketing information and training.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Roberto wants to find future networking events organized by the chamber of commerce to meet potential business partners and clients and expand his professional network.
  • Scenario 2: Lucas is interested in attending marketing-related workshops organized by the chamber of commerce, in order to further gain knowledge and network with other professionals in the area.
  • Scenario 3: Roberto is researching options for promoting his transportation company and is looking for resources on the chamber's website on effective marketing strategies for local start-up businesses.
  • Scenario 4: Lucas looks for opportunities to collaborate with other local businesses that offer complementary services, such as graphic designers or social media specialists, through the business directory on the chamber's website.
  • Scenario 5: Roberto is considering becoming a member of the chamber and wants to learn about benefits, membership fees, and how to join through the website.
